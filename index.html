Accelerate

High-Performance Parallel Arrays for Haskell

Accelerate is an embedded language for array-based computations in Haskell. Programs are expressed in the form of collective operations on regular multi-dimensional arrays, which are online compiled and executed on a range of architectures including multicore CPUs and GPUs.
